☕ Coffee Cup Roasters — Premium Specialty Coffee Website

A premium, modern, and fully responsive specialty coffee website designed to deliver an immersive café experience. The project combines elegant UI/UX, luxury coffee-inspired branding, interactive animations, online ordering, shopping cart functionality, and performance-focused front-end architecture.

✨ Overview

Coffee Cup Roasters is a high-end specialty coffee website inspired by modern coffee brands and boutique cafés. The website focuses on storytelling, product presentation, customer engagement, and seamless online ordering.

Key Goals
Showcase specialty coffee products professionally.
Create a luxury coffee brand experience.
Increase customer engagement through animations and interactive sections.
Provide a seamless ordering experience.
Maintain excellent performance and responsiveness across all devices.
