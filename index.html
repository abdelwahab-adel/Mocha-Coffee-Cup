Our Philosophy

A small, deliberate practice.

Mocha Coffee Cup began as a single drum roaster in the back of a bar on Brazil Street. We've stayed small on purpose — because great coffee is a slow, daily, uncompromising act.

We don't roast coffee. We curate the relationships between farmers, beans, water, and the people who drink them — one cup at a time.

From Origin to Cup

How the coffee gets to you.

Four steps, no shortcuts. The same ritual every Tuesday and Friday, scaled by hand, never by accident.

01

Source

Direct relationships with 11 farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, Guatemala, and Sumatra. Paid above fair-trade, every harvest.

02

Roast

A 12-kilo drum, profiled by hand for each lot. Roast curves logged, tasted, adjusted. Pulled at first crack.

03

Brew

Pulled on La Marzocco espresso, brewed on V60 or Kalita. Water at 93°C, 35 ppm, rested 12 hours.

04

Serve

In ceramic by Hasami Porcelain. Poured to the rim, served at the moment, never earlier, never reheated.
